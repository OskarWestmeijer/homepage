Values

Company culture is very important. I believe that sharing the same fundamental values with your colleagues, builds trust and general well-being at work.

Personally I put great emphasize into reliability, transparency, openness and honesty.

Making decisions

Solving a problem together as a team is always challenging. Everyone has his viewpoint and sometimes these just do not seem to align very well. I tend to ask my peers what is on their mind and how they would solve the situation. Ideally this opinion is shared with the whole team. This way everyone is heard, and we can gain a common understanding.

Based on these viewpoints we can decide in a rational and democratic way the best solution. Should I still disagree with the chosen solution, I will voice my concerns. But it is even more important to accept and trust the team decision. Only in extreme cases would I insist on a prolonged discussion.
